If you are looking for talk therapy, you are probably in the wrong place. Visit my professional website [Estrela Counseling](https://estrelacounseling.com).

Still here? This site is a collection of tangents, half-baked opinions, mediocre art, and the sort of curiosities that sensible people wisely ignore. Joshua Pritikin has nothing particularly important to say, and says it anyway, at length, with great confidence.

You would almost certainly be better off closing this tab. Go outside. Call a friend. Do literally anything else. The posts here are the intellectual equivalent of rummaging through someone else's junk drawer. You may occasionally find a pen that still works. There are also mystery keys to forgotten locks.
